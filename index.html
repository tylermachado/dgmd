Week 5

Early Project Mockup

I am doing everything backwards and have come up with form before function. What this demo does is use D3.js to populate a 50x50 square grid. Since it's D3, each of the little squares has one piece of data attached to it (a number from 0-2499). The fill is a random number but within certain bounds relating to that data number, so that I have enough control to preserve the green/blue gradient but enough randomness to create the dynamic moving effect.

I am happy with the general aesthetic -- it is something I have had vaguely in my head and that's why I jumped right into a code demo instead of sketching. That and I can't draw for shit. However I think the effect of just loading the page and letting random numbers do their thing is kind of unsatisfying. I would like to preserve the grid look and the "living" dynamism of it, but I'd like the visuals to be powered by data, or viewer editable, or something that has a little more meaning.
